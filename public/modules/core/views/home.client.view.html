Welcome

Our rally is put together by Walmart Associate Volunteers to raise much needed funds for the Children's Miracle Network Hospitals & Arkansas Children's Hospital. We've had tremendous success in the past rallies because of our gracious partners, dedicated volunteers, and visiting patrons.
